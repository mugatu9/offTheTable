About

The Problem:

You're having a dinner party and each of your guests has a unique set of food allergies and aversions.

The Solution:

Off the Table is an application that generates a report aggregating all of your guests' food allergies and aversions into an easily referenced list to assist you in planning a meal everyone can enjoy.
